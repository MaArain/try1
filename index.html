Welcome to Marcio Martins Baldino

Freelancer Designer of Custom Football Kits

At Marcio Martins Baldino, we specialize in creating custom football kits that capture the spirit of fans across Europe and beyond. Whether you're looking for a unique design for your local team or a personalized jersey that represents your passion, you've come to the right place!


Explore Our Collection

Check out our Gallery to view more of our creative designs. We love sharing our latest projects, and you can find inspiration for your own custom kit. Discover a wide range of fan-inspired football kits that are as vibrant and diverse as the teams we love. Please note that these are concept designs, not actual kits. If you see something you like or wish to order a custom design, do not hesitate to contact us or visit us in person.


Your Ideas Matter

Not finding the perfect kit? We are here to help! Feel free to chat with us and share your ideas. Our goal is to bring your vision to life and create a design that truly represents you. We take pride in our collaborative approach and strive to exceed your expectations.


Join Our Community

Stay connected with us on our social media channels to keep up with our latest designs, promotions, and news. Join our growing community of football enthusiasts!


Visit us

Want to see our kits up close? Come visit us in person! Our store is open from 08:00 AM to 05:00 PM, Monday through Saturday., and we would love to meet you. Experience our designs firsthand and discuss how we can create something special together.
